Our Journey

From humble beginnings to transformative impact in Northern Uganda.

Rooted in Resilience

Born from the resilient spirit of Northern Uganda, Apeiros Investments started as Ogur United Mothers (OGUM), a community-driven initiative formed in the aftermath of forced displacement. Inspired by the need to rebuild livelihoods post-conflict, OGUM empowered local farmers to pool resources, benefiting from National Agricultural Advisory Services (NAADS) to cultivate and market crops like upland rice and maize.

Over time, members expanded into bulk trading and local poultry production, laying the foundation for Apeiros Investments Limited, formally established in 2021. Today, we are a dynamic enterprise focused on sustainable agriculture, farmer empowerment, construction, and supplies, committed to scaling operations and driving real change across Uganda.
